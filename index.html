Biography

I’m an MSc Operational Research (OR) with Data Science graduate from the University of Edinburgh, UK. Projects

Bike Sharing
Designed model for rebalancing and scheduling of Just Eat bikes in Edinburgh through a combination of FICO Xpress and Python for implementation and R for historic analysis.
Laptop Manufacturing Process
Designed a simulation for a typical laptop manufacturing process using Simul8 and Excel for input and output analysis. Using testing and analysis, suggested improvements to increase the throughput and better the manufacturing process
Optimal allocation of population to vaccination centres
This project involved suggesting a time-dependent facility location and scheduling model such that every person within Scotland visits the nearest vaccination centre, according to a certain priority group and that the vaccinations are received according to age wise index and within a certain period as laid out by health board authorities (NHS Scotland).
Paediatric Vaccine Pricing
Conducted a mini case study on a paediatric vaccine pricing model for Centres of Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), using a combination of technologies such as NEOS server, BARON and Python to optimise the pricing of DTaP vaccines in private and public sectors in the US. Through a series of testing and running simulations, various strategies were developed for CDC in case of vaccine deficiency and market segmentation.
Predicting Cause of Diabetes
Analysed various biomarkers using health data to understand and predict the causes behind diabetes and eGFR over a period in R. Finally, fitted a regression model with the best set of predictors and performed 10 folds cross-validation to validate the AUC.
Predicting Hotel Cancellation
Analysed the reasons behind cancellation of hotel bookings through a series of exploratory data analysis, which was followed by feature engineering and various classification models. Achieved an accuracy of 0.82 through a random forest model implementation.
